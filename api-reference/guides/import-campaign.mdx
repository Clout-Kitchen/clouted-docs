---
title: 'Import clips into a campaign'
description: 'Attach a batch of clip URLs to a clipping campaign and confirm they were ingested.'
---

**Outcome:** attach a batch of clip URLs to the right clipping campaign and confirm they were
ingested.

<Note>
This is for **clipping campaigns** (`POST /campaigns/{uuid}/clips`). For tracking campaigns, use
[Import content into a tracking campaign](/api-reference/guides/import-tracking-campaign) — a
different endpoint with a different `status` enum.
</Note>

<Steps>
<Step title="Find the campaign uuid">
List campaigns and filter to the one you want. Use `search` and `status` to narrow it down.

```http
GET /campaigns?search=summer&status=active&pageSize=20
Authorization: Bearer <YOUR_TOKEN>
```

Read `data[].uuid` for the campaign whose `name` matches.
</Step>

<Step title="Import the clip URLs">
`POST` the URLs to that campaign. Both `urls` (1–100 URIs) and `status` are required.

```http
POST /campaigns/{uuid}/clips
Authorization: Bearer <YOUR_TOKEN>
Content-Type: application/json

{
  "urls": ["https://youtube.com/watch?v=abc", "https://tiktok.com/@x/video/123"],
  "status": "pending"
}
```

`status` must be one of `pending`, `healthy`, `suspicious`, `flagged`. Use `pending` for
new submissions you want reviewed.
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm ingestion">
List the campaign's clips to verify they landed and to read their metrics.

```http
GET /campaigns/{uuid}/clips?status=pending&platform=youtube&pageSize=50
Authorization: Bearer <YOUR_TOKEN>
```

Each item in `data` carries `url`, `platform`, `status`, the `clipper` (`{ uuid, username }`),
and `stats` (`views`, `likes`, `comments`, `uniqueViewers`).
</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Imports are capped at 100 URLs per call. Chunk larger sets and repeat the `POST`. A duplicate
submission or an unsupported-platform URL fails the request.
</Info>
</content>
